## 📌 Project Overview

> **Tea** is the second most consumed beverage in the world — but how does it travel from the farms of China and India to cups across the globe?

This project is my **IronViz 2026 Qualifier submission**, exploring the full lifecycle of global tea trade — from production volumes and domestic supply to international import/export flows and market pricing. The analysis uncovers which countries dominate production, which drive value, and where the biggest pricing disparities exist between volume-driven and value-driven markets.

## 🎯 Objectives

- 🌍 Analyze **global tea production** trends across major producing countries
- 🔄 Map **import–export trade flows** and identify key trading relationships
- 💰 Compare **trade volume (tonnes) vs. trade value (USD)** to spot pricing disparities
- 🏭 Identify countries that are **volume-driven** exporters vs. **value-driven** exporters
- 📊 Build an interactive **Tableau dashboard** for the IronViz 2026 competition

## 🔍 Methodology

### 1️⃣ Data Collection & Cleaning
- Sourced **multi-country datasets** covering tea production volumes, domestic supply, and import/export figures
- Cleaned and integrated disparate data sources in **SQL** — handling nulls, standardizing country names, and aligning units (tonnes vs. USD)

### 2️⃣ SQL Analysis
- Computed key metrics: **total production**, **export volume**, **export value**, **import dependency ratio**
- Compared **trade volume vs. trade value (USD)** across countries to identify pricing power
- Segmented countries into **volume-driven** (high quantity, lower price) vs. **value-driven** (lower quantity, premium price) exporters

### 3️⃣ Tableau Dashboard
- Built an **interactive Tableau dashboard** visualizing:
  - Top tea-producing nations and their output trends
  - Global trade flow maps (exporters → importers)
  - Volume vs. value pricing disparity charts
  - Key exporters, importers & market concentration highlights

| 💡 Insight | 📝 Finding |
|:----------|:----------|
| 🏆 Top Producer | China dominates global tea production by volume |
| 💎 Value Leaders | Certain smaller nations command premium pricing despite lower volumes |
| 🔄 Trade Concentration | A handful of countries account for the majority of global tea exports |
| 📉 Volume vs. Value Gap | Significant pricing disparity exists between bulk exporters and specialty markets |
| 🌍 Import Dependence | Several regions are heavily reliant on a single source country for tea imports |
